Lasagna

Description

This lasagna with your choice of meat sauce is a delicious addition to any home cook's repertiore.

You will find yourself regularly coming back to this recipe time and time again at the request of the whole family, who will surely be looking for seconds each time you make it.

Ingredients

  • 12 lasagna noodles
  • 1 pound of lean ground beef or sausage
  • 2 cloves garlic, chopped
  • 1 tsp dried oregano
  • 1/2 tsp garlic powder
  • Salt and ground black pepper to taste
  • 1 (16 oz.) package of cottage cheese or ricotta cheese
  • 1/2 cup shredded Parmesan cheese
  • 2 eggs
  • 4 1/2 cups tomato-basil pasta sauce
  • 2 cups shredded mozzarella cheese

Steps

  1. Preheat the oven to 350 degrees F (175 C)
  2. Bring a large pot of salted water to a boil. Add lasagna noodles and cook for 10 minutes or until al dente; drain. (If using no boil noodles, skip this step)
  3. While noodles cook, place ground beef, garlic, oregano, garlic powder, salt, and black pepper in a large skillet over medium heat; cook and stir until beef is crumbly and evenly browned, about 10 minutes.
  4. Add tomato-basil sauce to ground meat mixture.
  5. Mix cottage (or ricotta) cheese parmesean cheese, and eggs together in a large bowl until thoroughly combined.
  6. spread a few spoonfuls of the meat sauce on the bottom of a 9x13 baking dish.
  7. Lay 4 noodles side by side on top of the layer of sauce mixture. Alternate layering cheese mixture, meat sauce, and noodles, using approximately 1/3 of each ingredient at a time.
  8. Sprinkle top with mozzarella cheese and cover with aluminum foil.
  9. Bake in a preheated oven for 30 minutes. Remove the foil and bake for an additional 10 minutes until cheese has started to brown.
  10. Allow lasagna to rest for at least 10 minutes before slicing and serving.
