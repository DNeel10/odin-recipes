Honey Garlic Slow Cooker Chicken Thighs

Home

Description

This chicken is easy, delicious, and uses ingredients common in everyone's kitchen.

You can serve this with white rice and steamed or stir-fried vegetables for a delicious sweet and savory meal!

Ingredients

  • 4 boneless, skinless chicken thighs
  • 1/2cup soy sauce
  • 1/2 cup ketchup
  • 1/3 cup honey
  • 3 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 tsp dried basil
  • (optional) 1 tsp chili flakes>

Steps

  1. Lay chicken thighs into the bottom of a 4-quart slow cooker.
  2. Whisk soy sauce, ketchup, honey, garlic, and basil together in a bowl; pour over the chicken.
  3. Cook on Low for 6 hours.
