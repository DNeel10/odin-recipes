Spaghetti

Description

This spaghetti with sauce made of fresh tomatoes, onion, and basil is a delicious and easy meal to make when short on time.

The authentic flavors of this dish will make everyone eating it question where in italy you imported the sauce from!

Ingredients

  • 1 lb grape tomatoes
  • 1/4 cup fresh basil, roughly chopped
  • 1/2 medium onion, diced
  • Spaghetti noodles
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • Salt and ground black pepper to taste
  • 1/2 cup shredded Parmesan cheese
  • 2 Tbsp olive oil

Steps

  1. Bring a large pot of salted water to a boil. Add spaghetti noodles and cook for 10 minutes or until al dente; Reserve about a cup of the pasta water before fully draining the noodles.
  2. While noodles cook, saute onion and garlic in a large skillet over medium heat
  3. Once onions become translucent, add tomatoes to the pan and cover.
  4. After the tomatoes have softened, use a potato masher to smash the tomatoes. Continue to cook over medium heat until the tomatoes are fully softened.
  5. Add cooked spaghetti noodles directly to the pan with the tomato mixture and stir. Add a few tablespoons of the reserved cooking liquid while stiring the noodles until the sauce begins to thicken and cling to the noodles.
  6. Add the shredded parmesan cheese to the noodles and continue stirring, occasionally adding additional pasta water as needed to get sauce to desired consistency.
  7. Sprinkle basil onto pasta and serve.
